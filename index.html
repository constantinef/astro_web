Autonomous Astro Observatory

Your AI-powered window to the cosmos. We relentlessly scan the stars for anomalies and events, turning raw data into groundbreaking discoveries.

The Universe is Expanding—And So Is Its Data

Modern telescopes generate petabytes of data, far more than humans can analyze. Countless secrets are hidden in this deluge. Our project was born from a single question: How can we automate discovery?

The Autonomous Astro Observatory is a sophisticated, GenAI-powered system designed to be the modern astronomer's most powerful tool. It connects to public astronomical data streams, working 24/7 to ingest, process, and analyze information. It's not just about finding needles in a haystack; it's about understanding what they mean.

Core Capabilities

Our system is a comprehensive, end-to-end solution for automated astronomical research.

24/7 Data Ingestion

We continuously pull data from leading open-source astronomical feeds, including transient alerts, new imagery, and research papers, ensuring our knowledge is always up-to-date.

Intelligent Analysis

At our core is a powerful AI agent that cross-references data streams to identify anomalies, track objects, and flag statistically significant events that warrant further study.

Automated Publishing

When a key finding is identified, the system automatically publishes the essential details and context to a public-facing API, making discoveries accessible to everyone.

Explore the Discoveries

Our findings will be programmatically accessible. Dive into the data, build your own applications, and be part of the next wave of cosmic discovery.
